Introduction


I am a born Sri Lankan currently living in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. My mother land is a beautiful country with many diverse cultures, breathtaking scenary, rich history and astonishing sculptures, statues and buildings.


Sri Lanka is an island in the Indian Ocean off the southeast tip of India, and is about half the size of Alabama. Most of the land is flat and rolling,with mountains in the south-central region rise to over 8,000 ft (2,438 m).
